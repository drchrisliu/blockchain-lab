The smart contract deployment barrier
💸
Prohibitive development cost Smart contract development requires blockchain specialists charging $200–$400/hr — placing it out of reach for most organisations.
⚠️
Irreversible coding errors Manual smart contract coding introduces security vulnerabilities and logic bugs that can't be patched after deployment without full rewrites.
🔄

🔒
Vendor and platform lock-in Contracts written for one blockchain can't be ported — switching platforms means starting from scratch at full cost.

How It Works

From business process model to live blockchain in three steps

No blockchain expertise required. If your team can describe a workflow, DRAK can deploy it.

1
Model Your Process
Use any standard BPMN graphical tool to draw your business workflow and a DMN editor to specify decision tables and rules. Export the standard XML file — your modelling work is done.
2
Transform Automatically
DRAK's engine analyses your BPMN/DMN models using DE-HSM multi-modal processing, generates a blockchain-independent intermediate model, then produces smart contracts and a REST API ready for deployment.
3
Deploy & Execute
Deploy to Ethereum or Hyperledger Fabric — or both. Invoke the generated API with your trade documents and credentials to initiate transactions. DRAK manages synchronisation, privacy, and ACID compliance automatically.
